Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services, bookings, pricing, products, and more.

General

How often should I book a clean?

Weekly or fortnightly works best for most homes. Monthly is great for lighter-traffic homes. We can tailor a schedule to your needs.

Do I need to be home?

No—many clients provide access instructions. Your home will be locked when we leave. Please let us know any alarm details in advance.

What areas do you service?

We are based in Epsom, Bendigo and service nearby suburbs. See our Areas we cover page for details.

Pricing & bookings

How is pricing calculated?

Pricing is based on scope and time required. We provide clear quotes up front. Extras or added time are always checked with you first.

What’s your cancellation policy?

We kindly ask for 24 hours’ notice to reschedule or cancel. Late notice may incur a minimum call-out fee.

How do I book?

Use the Get a Quote form or call us on 0423 011 078. We’ll confirm time, scope, and pricing.

Products & equipment

Do you bring products and equipment?

Yes, we bring everything needed. If you prefer us to use your products, please let us know—happy to accommodate.

Are eco-friendly products available?

Yes—upon request. Tell us your preference and we’ll ensure suitable products are used in your home.

Are you insured?

Yes, we are insured for public liability. Please report any issues within 24 hours so we can make it right promptly.
